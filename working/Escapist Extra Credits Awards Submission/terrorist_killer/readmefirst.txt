========================================================================
Terrorist Killer
========================================================================

------------------------------------------------------------------------
CONTROLS
------------------------------------------------------------------------

This is a one button game. You can use space, left mouse button, or 
joystick button one to control.

F12 will toggle debug mode, which will show you your FPS, and how long
you've been playing the game. It will also enable you to skip between
levels by pressing "N" and "P". 


------------------------------------------------------------------------
COMPLETION
------------------------------------------------------------------------

Play the game to completion for an end message, after the high score 
table.
